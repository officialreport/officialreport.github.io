About This Website

Hello, my name is Andre, I'm the owner of this website. This is a recreation of the original Official Report website, made back in approximately 2002 for the movie Zero Day. It is also heavily inspired by another recreation, however this one is unfinished and hasn't been updated in two years. As it is missing large chunks of recreatable content, I wanted to make my own version. This recreation is not 100% faithful, I am taking stylistic liberties. I am also coding this from the ground up, this is not based on source code from the original website or the Neocities recreation.
